BOOKS


    Mein Kampf

  • Author: Adolf Hitler

    • Madman, tyrant, animal—history has given Adolf Hitler many names. In Mein Kampf (My Struggle), often called the Nazi bible, Hitler describes his life, frustrations, ideals, and dreams.



    Wings of Fire

  • Author: Dr.A P J Abdul Kalam

    • Wings of Fire is an Autobiography of A P J Abdul Kalam, former President of India.Kalam examines his early life, effort, hardship, fortitude, luck and chance that eventually led him to lead Indian space research, nuclear and missile programs.



    The story of My Experiments with Truth

  • Author:Mahathma Gandhi

    • The Story of My Experiments with Truth is the autobiography of Mohandas K. Gandhi, covering his life from early childhood through to 1921.



    I am Nujood, Age 10 and Divorced

  • Author: Nujood Ali

    • Nujood tells of abuse at her husband's hands and of her daring escape. With the help of local advocates and the press, Nujood obtained her freedom—an extraordinary achievement in Yemen, where almost half of all girls are married under the legal age.



    Steve Jobs

  • Author:Walter Isaacson

    • Walter Isaacson's "enthralling" (The New Yorker) worldwide bestselling biography of Apple cofounder Steve Jobs.
